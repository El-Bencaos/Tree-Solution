Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is your site visit really free? ▼

Yes — completely free, with no obligation whatsoever. One of our certified arborists will visit your property, assess your trees, and provide a detailed quote. You decide whether or not to proceed.

How quickly can you respond? ▼

We typically respond to enquiries within a few hours and can usually arrange a site visit within 2–5 business days. For emergency callouts, we aim to be on site within 2 hours, 24/7.

Are you insured? ▼

Yes — we carry £5 million public liability insurance and full employer's liability insurance. You can request certificates at any time and we're happy to provide them before work begins.

What areas do you cover? ▼

We cover London and the wider South East of England, including Surrey, Kent, Essex, Hertfordshire, Berkshire, Sussex, and Oxfordshire. Not sure if we cover your area? Just get in touch — we'll let you know.

Do I need planning permission to remove a tree? ▼

It depends. Trees in Conservation Areas or those protected by a Tree Preservation Order (TPO) require prior consent from your local council. Our team can advise you on the rules for your specific tree and location, and we handle the applications on your behalf where needed.

Who We Are

Tree Care Is Not Just Our Job — It's Our Calling

Tree Solutions Ltd was founded in 2009 by Marcus Green, a passionate arborist with over two decades of hands-on experience. What started as a one-man operation with a single van has grown into a trusted team of 12 certified professionals serving hundreds of clients across London and the South East.

We believe trees are living monuments — they clean the air, shelter wildlife, and shape the character of every neighbourhood. Our mission is to care for them with the knowledge, respect, and precision they deserve.

Every job we take on — whether it's a single garden tree or a large estate — receives the same attention to detail, the same commitment to safety, and the same genuine passion for what we do.

Our Core Values

🌿
Environmental Responsibility
We recycle 100% of green waste — chipping into mulch, milling timber, and composting. We plant a tree for every significant removal.
🛡️
Safety Above All
Every team member holds up-to-date safety certifications. We follow strict protocols and carry full public liability and employer's insurance.
🤝
Honest & Transparent
No hidden costs. No upselling work that isn't needed. We give clear, honest assessments and stick to the price we quote.
🎓
Continuous Learning
The world of arboriculture evolves. Our team undergoes regular training and CPD to stay at the forefront of best practice.
